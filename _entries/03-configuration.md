Daze doesn't have a lot of mandatory configuration. On the other hand, everything is configurable and this are the options:

## `daze.yml`

```
entriesPath: _entries
themesPath: .daze/themes
routes:
    entry: /{slug}/
theme: daze
title: 'Daze Documentation'
```
